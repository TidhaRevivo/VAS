    Salametkom (Your Safety) - ‏The organization “Your Safety” started with a simple idea!

    It is represented in providing assistance to Arab patients in Israeli hospitals, especially in translation!

    But within a short period, "Your Safety" became an important address, and the only one in most cases,
    in many hospitals in Jerusalem and the Center,

    in order to provide assistance to thousands of Palestinian patients from the West Bank and Gaza:
    in medical coverage, transportation, medical equipment and tools, in addition to recreational activities inside and outside hospitals.
