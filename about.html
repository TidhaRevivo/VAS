    • Who are we?

    We are a team of students from a program named Meet. Meet is a program which combines Israeli and Palestinian students in order to learn Computer science and entrepreneurship.During our yearly program, we were given an assignment in which we had to find a solution to a problem that bothers us. What bothered us the most is the lack of people who volunteer around us! This is why we created a website where every person who wants to volunteer but doesn't know where- can find the perfect place for them to have fun and give back to their community!




    What is our vision?

    We believe that volunteering is extremelty important in every community, but is often over looked. We believe that by making volunteering accessable, people would volunteer more. We hope that through our website people will be able to find the place most suitable for them, thus making volunteering something enjoyable! Something people want to do.







    How does it work?

    Take a short quiz about your personality and prefrences and in result get information and a way to contact the place that suits you the most. In adittion, you can read stories from people who volunteered in that place.
