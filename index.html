    • What Is Our Purpose?

    We believe that volunteering is something no community can live without, volunteering is something that shapes a community. After doing some research we learnt that many people want to volunteer but dont know where or how. This is why we are here! In our website you will find the most suitable volunteering place for you and an easy way for you to get in contact with it.

    How Does It Work?

    This is the simplest thing you can do! All you have to do is follow three easy steps:
    1. take a short quiz about your personality and prefrences
    2. Contact the place that we suggested if it is to your liking
    3. GO VOLUNTEER!
